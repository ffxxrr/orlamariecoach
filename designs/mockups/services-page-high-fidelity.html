Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our meditation services

What if I've never meditated before?
+
Perfect! Most of my clients are complete beginners. I specialize in making meditation accessible and approachable, regardless of your experience level. We'll start with simple, practical techniques that you can immediately apply in your daily life. There's no pressure to sit in uncomfortable positions or empty your mind completely ‚Äì that's not what meditation is about.
How do I know which service is right for me?
+
I offer a free 15-minute consultation where we'll discuss your goals, current stress levels, and learning preferences. Based on this conversation, I'll recommend the best approach for your situation. You can also start with a single 1-on-1 session to get a feel for working together before committing to a course or workshop series.
Can I switch between services?
+
Absolutely! Many clients find that their needs evolve as their practice develops. You might start with individual sessions to build confidence, then join online courses for structured learning, and later participate in group workshops for community support. I'm here to guide you to the most appropriate service for your current stage of growth.
What's your refund policy?
+
I offer a satisfaction guarantee for all services. If you're not completely satisfied with your first individual session, I'll provide a full refund. For courses and workshops, you have 7 days from the start date to request a full refund if the content isn't meeting your needs. My goal is your growth and peace of mind, both in practice and in our business relationship.
