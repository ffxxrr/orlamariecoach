A Journey from Ancient Wisdom

From a teenage awakening to 30 years of dedicated practice, discover the path that shaped the Orla Marie Method.

🌱
The Awakening (Age 16)

My spiritual journey began at 16 with a profound awakening that changed everything. Drawn to the ancient wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita, I discovered meditation not as a trend, but as a timeless practice connecting us to our deepest truth.

"When the student is ready, the teacher appears. The Bhagavad Gita became my first guide into the depths of consciousness."

This early introduction to traditional meditation practices laid the foundation for three decades of continuous learning, practice, and eventually, sharing this wisdom with others.

🍀
Donegal Roots & Ancient Sites

Growing up in the mystical landscape of Donegal, surrounded by ancient stone circles and megalithic sites, I was naturally drawn to the spiritual heritage of Ireland. This connection to the land and its ancient wisdom became integral to my teaching approach.

"The stones of Donegal have witnessed thousands of years of human seeking. They remind us that this path is both ancient and eternal."

My Irish heritage isn't just background—it's woven into every meditation I guide, bringing Celtic spirituality together with Eastern wisdom traditions.

🎓
Professional Training & The Orla Marie Method

After decades of personal practice, I pursued formal training with an MA in Counselling and specialised meditation certifications. This unique combination allows me to offer both the depth of traditional practice and the understanding of modern psychological approaches.

"True meditation teaching requires both ancient wisdom and modern understanding of how we heal and grow."

The Orla Marie Method emerged from this synthesis—personalised meditation practices that honour traditional wisdom while addressing the unique challenges of contemporary life.

The Spirit of Donegal

My work is deeply rooted in the mystical landscape of Donegal, where ancient stone circles and megalithic sites have been places of contemplation for thousands of years.

The land teaches us that wisdom is both ancient and ever-present, waiting to be discovered in the quiet moments of our lives.

This connection to Irish heritage brings a unique Celtic perspective to meditation practice, honouring both the contemplative traditions of the East and the spiritual wisdom of the Celtic lands.

When you work with me, you're not just learning meditation techniques—you're connecting with a lineage of seekers who have found peace and wisdom in these practices across cultures and centuries.
