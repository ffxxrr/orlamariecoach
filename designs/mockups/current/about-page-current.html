A Lifetime Dedicated to Practice

From early spiritual awakening to developing The OM Method, discover the journey that created a unique approach to meditation and mindfulness.

My spiritual journey began in my teenage years when I was first drawn to meditation and mindfulness practices. What started as personal exploration has evolved into three decades of dedicated practice and over ten years of guiding others on their own transformative paths.

Growing up in the mystical landscape of Donegal, I was naturally connected to the spiritual heritage of Ireland. The ancient stone circles and sacred sites of our land became my first teachers, showing me that the search for inner peace and wisdom is both timeless and universal. This deep connection to Celtic spirituality continues to influence my approach to teaching.

"True meditation isn't about escaping life—it's about finding the courage to be fully present with whatever arises."

My formal education includes an MA in Counselling, which provides the psychological foundation for understanding how meditation and mindfulness create lasting change. This academic background, combined with decades of personal practice, allows me to offer both ancient wisdom and modern therapeutic insights.

I hold certifications in multiple meditation traditions and am specifically qualified in Mindfulness for Kids, recognising that these life-changing practices can benefit people of all ages. My training in energy healing adds another dimension to the transformative work we can do together.

The OM Method

Through years of practice and teaching, I've developed The OM Method—a personalised approach that combines traditional meditation wisdom with contemporary understanding of how we heal and grow. Each person's journey is unique, and my role is to help you discover the practices that resonate most deeply with your own path to inner peace.

Working with students from all walks of life has taught me that whilst the techniques may be ancient, their application must be relevant to modern challenges. Whether you're dealing with stress, seeking clarity, or simply wanting to deepen your spiritual practice, I'm here to guide you with warmth, understanding, and genuine care for your wellbeing.

My online platform allows me to connect with people across all time zones, making these transformative practices accessible regardless of where you are in the world. From the hills of Donegal to wherever your journey takes you, we can work together to cultivate the peace and clarity you're seeking.
