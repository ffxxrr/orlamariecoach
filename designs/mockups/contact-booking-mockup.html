Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started with confidence.

How quickly will I hear back after contacting you? +
I typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours, often sooner. You'll receive a personalized response addressing your specific questions and next steps.
What if I'm completely new to meditation? +
Perfect! I specialize in working with complete beginners. My approach is gentle, practical, and designed to meet you exactly where you are in your journey.
How do online sessions work? +
Sessions are conducted via secure video call using Digital Samba. You'll receive a link 24 hours before your session, along with preparation guidance to ensure you get the most from our time together.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I accept payments through Revolut and PayPal, both of which support major credit cards and bank transfers from anywhere in the world. Payment is secure and processed immediately upon booking.
