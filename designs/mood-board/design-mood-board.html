Implementation Strategy

Visual Hierarchy

  • Crimson Pro for major headings and emotional content
  • Inter for body text, navigation, and functional elements
  • Generous white space for breathing room
  • Progressive disclosure to prevent overwhelm

Layout Philosophy

  • Journey-based navigation reflecting personal growth
  • Soft, rounded corners for approachability
  • Subtle gradients and shadows for depth
  • Nature-inspired organic spacing

Content Strategy

  • Trust elements prominently displayed
  • Social proof integrated naturally
  • Clear value propositions
  • Gentle calls-to-action
