# Content Directory

This directory will contain all website content including:

## Structure

- `pages/` - Content for individual pages
- `copy/` - Marketing copy and website text
- `images/` - Content images and photos
- `testimonials/` - Client testimonials and reviews
- `courses/` - Content for online meditation courses

## Content Guidelines

- All content should reflect Orla's calm, professional approach to meditation
- Use language that is accessible to beginners while maintaining expertise
- Focus on benefits and transformation rather than just techniques
- Include calls-to-action that guide visitors toward booking or course enrollment

## Review Process

All content will be reviewed with Orla before implementation to ensure:
- Accuracy of meditation techniques and philosophy
- Alignment with her personal brand and voice
- Compliance with any relevant industry standards
